Creamy Chicken Enchiladas


Description

A simple and tasty "poor person" baked chicken enchiladas recipe.
Easy to double the recipe to serve a crowd.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • ½ cup chopped green onions
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 (10.5 ounce) can low sodium condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 (4 ounce) can diced green chiles
  • ½ cup sour cream
  • 1 ½ cups cubed cooked chicken breast meat
  • 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese, divided
  • 6 (12 inch) flour tortillas
  • ¼ cup milk
