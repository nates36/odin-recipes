Millie's Pizza

Description

This classic recipe developed by Millie in 2022 melds the delicious flavors of tomatoes, cheese, and crust. It is fantastic.

She made it based on ingredients her dad loves.

Ingredients

  • Premade pizza dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Olives
  • Tomatoes
  • Mushrooms

Steps

  1. Spread tomato sauce on the pizza dough.
  2. Sprinkle cheese and add toppings.
  3. Bake in 400 degree oven for 10 minutes or until cheese bubbles and crust is golden.
