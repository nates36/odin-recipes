Nate's Cereal

Description

This amazing cereal recipe was perfected over thousands of years.

It was passed on to him from his ancestors.

Ingredients

  • Cereal
  • Milk
  • Bowl
  • Spoon

Steps

  1. Pour cereal in the bowl.
  2. Pour milk over cereal. NEVER DO THESE STEPS IN REVERSE!!!
  3. Consume with spoon.
