Jen's Grilled Cheese

Description

This classic recipe was perfected during Jen's time overseas studying the grazing habits of the wild bread cow.

You can't eat just one.

Ingredients

  • Sliced bread
  • Cheese
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Butter the outsides of the bread.
  2. Place cheese slices between 2 slices of bread.
  3. Grill in pan until cheese is melty and bread is toasted.
